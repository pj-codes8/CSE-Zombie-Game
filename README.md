# CSE-Zombie-Game

## Group Members: Diego Bernal and Patrick Ordona

## Description
For our midterm for CSE 4410 Game Programming we made a wavebased zombie survival game. The goal of the game is to kill all of the zombies and survive all of the waves. Currently there are 2 waves. The player can move using the WASD keys and turn their head with the mouse. To shoot the player can left click and to switch weapons the player can use the mouse scroll to switch between the 3 weapons. The 3 weapons are a pistol, shotgun, and an AK-47. Enemies will patrol and chase the player. When the player is in range the zombie will inflict damage on to them. When the zombie dies they have a chance of droppign ammo. To reload the weapons the player can press the 'R' key to reload.
